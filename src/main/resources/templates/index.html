What types of transactions can I perform using the app?

Our app enables a wide range of transactions, including fund transfers, bill payments, mobile recharges, peer-to-peer transfers, and more. You can also monitor your account balance and transaction history in real-time.

How secure is my financial information and transactions?

We prioritize your security. Our app uses advanced encryption and security protocols to safeguard your personal and financial data. We also offer two-factor authentication (2FA) options for an added layer of protection.

Are there any fees associated with using the app?

While many features of the app are free, certain transactions or services may incur fees. These fees will be clearly communicated before you proceed with the transaction, ensuring transparency.

How quickly are transactions processed?

Most transactions are processed instantly, ensuring that your funds are available when you need them. However, some transfers might take a bit longer due to factors such as bank processing times.

Can I link my bank account to the app?

Yes, you can link your bank account to the app for seamless fund transfers. We use secure connections to ensure the safety of your financial information.

What is your opening time?

We're pleased to offer round-the-clock service, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Our commitment to accessibility means you can access our services, support, and assistance whenever you need them, no matter the time zone or location. Whether it's daytime or nighttime, we're here to provide uninterrupted support and ensure a seamless experience for all our users.

What should I do if I encounter a problem with a transaction?

If you experience any issues with a transaction, reach out to our customer support team. We're available [business hours/timezone] to assist you and resolve any concerns you may have.

Is there a limit on how much I can transact using the app?

Depending on your account type and verification status, there might be transaction limits. You can find this information in the app or on our website. If you need higher limits, you can request an upgrade with the necessary documentation.
